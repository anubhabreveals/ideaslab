Kausik Bhattacharya

PhD Student
kausikb@iisc.ac.in
+91 9886086530

Kausik has more than 20 years of experience developing technology-driven products and service solutions for global markets. An engineering graduate from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, Kausik is analytical, process oriented and has strong academic credentials. After graduation, Kausik worked in multiple organizations in engineering and healthcare domains with deep hands-on experience in Product and Service Design. Kausik worked on technical systems design, services design, design optimization, design informatics and user experience design. Apart from product design, Kausik has passion and experience in research and talent development activities. In Aug 2019, Kausik joined CPDM at the Indian Institute of Science to build research experience in design innovation and design of smart products using analogical reasoning and artificial intelligence. Kausik is a recipient of National Merit awards, Prof G C Sen Memorial award, IGMDP Fellowship (Govt of India), and Prime Minister Research Fellowship (Govt of India). Kausik also received several corporate and management awards, including the Johnson & Johnson Standard of Leadership Award. Kausik has more than 20 technical reports and technical papers to his credit.

Specialties: Design Thinking, Design Creativity, Design Research, User Experience Design, Human-Computer Interactions, AI and Machine Learning, Automation, Software Engineering, Process Re-Engineering, Six Sigma.
